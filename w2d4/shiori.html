Background properties

Thebackgroundshorthand CSS property sets all background style properties at once, such as color, image, origin and size, or repeat method.

Property Description
background-size The size of the element's background image. The image can be left to its natural size, stretched, or constrained to fit the available space.
background-clip Whether an element's background extends underneath its border box, padding box, or content box.
background-color The background color of an element.
background-image One or more background images on an element.
background-origin The background's origin: from the border start, inside the border, or inside the padding.
background-repeat How background images are repeated. A background image can be repeated along the horizontal and vertical axes, or not repeated at all.
background-position The initial position for each background image. The position is relative to the position layer set by background-origin.
background-attachment Whether a background image's position is fixed within the viewport, or scrolls with its containing block.

Property values

background-color

color: red, #ff0000, #f00, rgb(255, 0, 0), hsl(hue, saturation, lightness)
The uniform color of the background. It is rendered behind any background-image that is specified, although the color will still be visible through any transparency in the image.

background-image

none:
Is a keyword denoting the absence of images.
image: url(URL or address of the image)
Is an "image" denoting the image to display. There can be several of them, separated by commas, as multiple backgrounds are supported.
