TRANSFORM TUTORIAL

What is "transform"?

transform CSS property lets you rotate, scale, skew, or translate an element.

transform rotate

transform: rotate(1080deg);
transition-duration: 3s;

In rotate, you can rotate anything you want. In the example on the right side, I set 1080 deg which is three times of 360, so this object on the right side will rotate three times within 3 seconds.

hover your mouse over on the cat :)

transform skew

transform: skewX(50deg);
transition-duration: 1s;

In skew, you can skew anything you want. In the example on the right side, I set 50 deg, so the object will skew 50 degree within 1 second.
